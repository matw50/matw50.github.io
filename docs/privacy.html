Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 31, 2025

Thank you for using Sommify ("we", "us", or "our"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS app, which captures images of wine menus and uses cloud-based machine learning to analyze and rate wines.

1. Information We Collect

a. Image Data
When you use the app to take a picture of a wine menu, the image is temporarily uploaded to our secure cloud servers to be analyzed by our AI model. We do not associate these images with your personal identity.

b. Usage Data
We may collect anonymous usage data, such as how often the app is used, types of menus scanned, and interaction patterns, to help us improve user experience.

c. Device Information
We collect limited technical data, such as your device type, operating system version, and app version, solely for diagnostics and analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and analyze wine menu images.
  • Deliver annotated results back to your device.
  • Improve the accuracy and performance of our AI models.
  • Enhance app functionality and fix bugs.

3. Data Storage and Retention

  • Uploaded images are temporarily stored on our cloud servers and are deleted within 24 hours after processing.
  • No personally identifiable information is stored with your images.
  • Usage and diagnostic data may be retained in anonymized form for analytical purposes.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • With trusted cloud service providers strictly for processing (e.g., image recognition, storage).
  • If required by law or to respond to legal process.

5. Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your data using industry-standard encryption during transmission and while temporarily stored on our servers. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Sommify is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Choices

  • You may delete the app at any time to stop any further data collection.
  • You can request data deletion or ask questions by contacting us at: info@spectrify.co.uk

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or the App Store listing.
