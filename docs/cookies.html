Cookie Policy

Effective Date: March 31, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how Spectrify ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies in connection with our Sommify application and our website.

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website or use certain mobile applications. They are widely used to make websites and applications work more efficiently and provide valuable information to the owners.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the functioning of our application and website. They enable core features such as security, account management, and remembering user preferences. You cannot opt out of these cookies as the application and website cannot function properly without them.

2.2 Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how users interact with our application and website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This helps us improve our services based on user behavior patterns.

2.3 Performance Cookies

These cookies collect information about how you use our application and website, such as which pages you visit most often and if you receive any error messages. They do not collect information that identifies you directly.

3. Mobile Application Data Storage

In our mobile application, we may use technologies similar to cookies, such as local storage or application data caches, to store user preferences and to improve user experience by storing certain information locally on your device.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Our application and website may include cookies from third-party services such as:

  • Analytics providers (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Cloud service providers for image processing

These third parties may use cookies when you use our application or visit our website, and we do not control these cookies. Please refer to the respective privacy policies of these third parties for more information.

5. Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to manage your cookie preferences. You can set your browser to refuse cookies or delete certain cookies. Generally, you can also manage similar technologies in your mobile device settings.

Please note that if you choose to block cookies, you may not be able to use the full functionality of our application or website.

6. Changes to this Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, regulation, or our business practices. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
